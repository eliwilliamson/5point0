Front-end Developer

DUSK is looking for a front-end developer who’s passionate about markup – and its role in making things that matter. At DUSK our front-end developers are an integral part of our design team and design process. We believe that semantic integrity, performance and extensibility are as critical to design as aesthetics.

Help us build better products.

What we’re looking for

Concepts like “seniority” and “experience” are less important to us than raw skill, talent and a hunger to learn and grow. If our ideal candidate description below resonates with you, read our handbooks and get in touch with us.

Desired skills and experience

Our ideal candidate

  • You are proficient in writing valid, semantic HTML, CSS (including Flexbox), and have some JavaScript experience
  • You’re familiar with HTML style guides / pattern libraries and writing modular, reusable code
  • You’re a thinker and can contribute to problem solving design oriented user experience issues
  • You have a keen eye for details when it comes to design, performance, browser compatibility
  • You’re familiar with the concepts like progressive enhancement, mobile-first, and responsive design
  • You’re excited by the possibility of working closely with designers, product strategists and other developers in a team
  • The ability to write well tested and clean code
  • You have experience with version control (ideally GitHub) and understand (or are willing to learn) best practises around managing, sharing and deploying your code
  • You’re responsible and self motivated, you can manage your own workloads and daily schedule
