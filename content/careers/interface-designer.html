Interface Designer

DUSK is looking for a skilled interface designer to help us create digital products that people love using. If you approach design with the intent to solve problems and you love shaping what a product does – not just what it looks like – then you’ll love working at DUSK.

Help us design better products.

What we’re looking for

Kyle Steed said: “If you’re not solving a problem, you’re not designing.” At DUSK, we’re of the opinion that designing is a process of distilling clarity. Design solves problems. Good design solves problems elegantly. We’re looking for people who are as passionate about how their design feels, performs and transacts with users, as they are about the way it looks.

Desired skills and experience

Our ideal candidate

  • Has demonstrable experience, preferably more than 2 years, designing interfaces for interactive web services and software
  • Is proficient in the web design process and experienced with software like Adobe Creative Suite – i.e. you understand how to work with layers, vectors, bitmaps and the like
  • Is able to contribute through all phases of the product design process, from the strategic process, through to wireframing, prototyping and supplying assets to developers
  • Understands concepts like user experience, performance, usability and has a portfolio of work that displays an ability to design useful, communicative interfaces
  • Works well in a team and finds the idea of designing collaboratively with developers, product strategists, clients and users, appealing
  • Has an understanding of or experience with creating or editing HTML and CSS – the more, the better
  • Is passionate about technology and finding more efficient and better ways of doing things
