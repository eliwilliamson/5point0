Working with us

Made to make a difference

We’re a product company. We build things that do things. We build things that help people get things done. We’re not a digital marketing agency. We build interfaces and technology that serve a business purpose. People who work here enjoy the act that they’re building things that have an impact on businesses, or, people’s lives.

Be productive

We place a significant amount of effort into increasing our productivity. We work in the cloud so you can work faster, more collaboratively, with on–the–fly reviews and asynchronous processes. Less meetings, less distractions means more working – which is awesome when you love what you do and want to do more of it. We embrace Lean Startup and Agile processes. We move fast with a bias towards building. At the end of the day we’re output and productivity focused, not hours-bound.

Work wherever work gets done

At the end of the day, what really matters is what gets made, not where it gets made. Because we want you to make awesome stuff, and lots of it, we’re structured for both office-based and remote working. Work from the sanctity of your home where pants are optional. Or, work from DUSK HQ where we have a stocked fridge, good coffee, nice people and decent enough beer. Whether it’s our place, your place or any place – we’ll let you work wherever you get work done.

Your career here

We want everyone who works with us to be wealthy, contented and growing in every sense of the word. We’re invested in our staff’s growth as people, employees and professionals. That looks different for everyone, but here’s some of the ways we look after our people:

5 working days additional learning leave per year
Performance bonuses
Investment in staff passion projects
Medical Aid and Retirement Annuity contributions
